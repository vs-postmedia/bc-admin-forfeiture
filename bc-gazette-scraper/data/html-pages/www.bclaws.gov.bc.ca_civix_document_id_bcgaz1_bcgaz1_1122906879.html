TAKE NOTICE THAT on 12/FEB/2020 an order was made for service on you of a notice of civil claim issued from the Victoria Registry of the Supreme Court of British Columbia in proceeding number 19 5634 by way of this advertisement.

In the proceeding, the plaintiff, Director of Civil Forfeiture, claims the following relief against you: forfeiture of a 2011 BMW 323i with Vehicle Identification Number WBAPG7C50BA936737 (the “Vehicle”), $205.50 in Canadian currency (the “Money”) and an Apple iPhone (the “Cellphone”) seized by the Richmond RCMP on August 22, 2019, and their proceeds.

You may obtain, from the Victoria Registry, at 850 Burdett Avenue, Victoria, BC, a copy of the notice of civil claim and the order providing for service by this advertisement.
