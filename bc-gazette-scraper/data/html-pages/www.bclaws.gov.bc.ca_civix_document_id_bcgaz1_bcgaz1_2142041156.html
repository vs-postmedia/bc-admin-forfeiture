PUBLIC NOTICE

To all persons who may have been involved in the following:

You may be entitled to share in moneys recovered by the Director of Civil Forfeiture arising from the case of Director v. $12,000 CAD and Clapper.

We cannot pay you any interest or gains on funds that were lost. Compensation is limited to your actual loss and does not cover pain or suffering. Also, if you already received compensation in relation to this incident, you must tell us about that in your application.

To make a claim, you, or someone who acts for you, must submit an application form and copies of all documentation pertaining to the loss.

You may send the application form and documents to us by mail, courier, fax or e-mail. Our contact information and instructions for obtaining the application form are located at the bottom of this ad. Please be aware that we cannot consider claims received after the close of business on November 29, 2018.

If you need an application, we will send you one. Call us toll free at 1-844-356-1560, e-mail us at civilfo@gov.bc.ca, or write us at PO Box 9234 Stn Prov Govt, Victoria, BC V8W 9J1. [se06]
